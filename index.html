Our Pizza Ethos

Fresh Dough

Our dough is made daily by our chefs using a combination of 00 5 Stagioni Napoletana flour with added whole-wheat to give a richer flavour. No chemicals, no additives, NO messing, we believe that fresh dough and clean ingredients make better pizza, simple!

Tomato Sauce

We make our tomato sauce daily to give a balanced, sweet and sour taste. It’s full of plump freshness with a low acidity and sweet tomato taste.

Mozzarella cheese

We use a For Di Latte (cow’s milk mozzarella) which is produced in Southern Italy and offers a smooth, extremely fresh, tangy flavour and has superb elastically.
